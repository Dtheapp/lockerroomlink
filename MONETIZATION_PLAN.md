**Our solution:**

**Revenue Model:**
| Type | Our Fee | Notes |
|------|---------|-------|
| Deal Facilitation | 10% of contract value | Split or charged to company |
| Featured Listing | $49/mo | Companies pay to be highlighted |
| Verified Badge | $9.99/year | Players pay for verification |

**Deal Examples:**
- Local pizza shop sponsors #22 for $200/month ‚Üí We get $20/mo
- Car dealership pays for team appearance $1000 ‚Üí We get $100
- Sports drink company pays for social post $50 ‚Üí We get $5

**Why this is HUGE:**
- NIL is expanding to high school in many states
- No good marketplace exists for youth/HS level
- First mover advantage
- Data moat (player stats, engagement)
