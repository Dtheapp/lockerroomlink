Welcome to Your Team

A simple, step-by-step guide for parents — from creating your account to adding your athlete and unlocking everything your team has to offer.

New here? You’re in the right place. This short guide walks you through joining OSYS, creating your athlete’s profile, and getting connected with your team. It takes about 5 minutes.

👉 Get started at osys.team
Part 1

🚀 Create Your Account

Get signed up as a parent so you can manage your athlete and stay connected.

1

Go to osys.team & tap “Get Started”

In your web browser, visit osys.team. On the welcome screen, tap Get Started to begin creating a new account.

2

Choose “Parent” as your role

Select Parent so your account is set up to add and manage your child’s athlete profile.

3

Enter your details

Provide your name, email, and a secure password. This is your account — your athlete will have their own profile that you manage.

4

Install the app to your phone (recommended)

When prompted, tap Add to Home Screen so OSYS behaves like a real app — faster access and push notifications.

📱 iPhone: Share → “Add to Home Screen”. Android: tap “Install”.
Part 2

🏈 Add Your Athlete

Create your child’s profile so a coach can add them to the team roster.

1

Go to “My Athletes”

From your dashboard or profile, find the My Athletes section and tap + Add Athlete.

2

Fill in the basics

Enter your athlete’s first and last name, date of birth, and gender. A nickname is optional and shows on their player card.

🎂 Date of birth sets the correct age group automatically.
3

Add a photo (recommended)

Tap the camera icon to upload a profile picture (max 2MB). You can change or remove it anytime from the athlete’s edit screen.

4

Share the username with your coach

Each athlete gets a unique @username. Your coach searches this to add your athlete to the team roster — so send it their way!

🔎 Coaches search by @username or name to add players.
5

You’re on the team!

Once your coach adds your athlete, you’ll get a notification and instantly unlock your team’s tools below.

Part 3

✨ What Your Team Offers

Once your athlete is on a roster, here’s everything you can do inside OSYS.

💬

Team Chat

Message coaches and other parents in one place. Get quick answers, coordinate rides, and never miss an update.

📅

Schedule & Calendar

See practices, games, and events with dates, times, and locations. Practices even include a full time-block itinerary.

🔔

Push Notifications

Turn on alerts for game days, schedule changes, and announcements — delivered right to your phone.

👥

Team Roster

View your team’s players and coaching staff so you always know who’s who.

📝

Registrations

Sign up for seasons, camps, and events, and handle any required forms or payments securely.

📊

Stats & Highlights

Follow your athlete’s stats and celebrate their progress throughout the season.

🎬

Film Room

Watch game and practice film shared by coaches to help your athlete improve.

❤️

Fan Hub & Profile

Share your athlete’s public profile with family and fans who want to cheer them on.

⭐ Quick Tips to Get the Most Out of OSYS

  • Install the app to your home screen and turn on notifications.
  • Keep your athlete’s date of birth accurate — it drives age-group placement.
  • Check the schedule before each week so you never miss a practice or game.
  • Say hi in Team Chat — it’s the fastest way to reach your coach.
