## Executive Summary

LockerRoomLink is a **feature-rich youth sports platform** with exceptional bones. The app is ~65% ready to become the operating system every youth sports organization depends on.

**What we're building:**
- The **Shopify** of youth sports (all commerce in one place)
- The **Stripe** of youth sports (all payments through us)  
- The **LinkedIn** of youth sports (NIL marketplace, coach hiring)
- The **ESPN** of youth sports (leagues, standings, AI stats)

## Recommendations

### Immediate (Before Pilot)
1. Add multi-sport support (sport type on teams)
2. Add error monitoring (Sentry)
3. Add basic analytics (PostHog)
4. Create onboarding flow
5. Add feedback button
6. Full testing pass

### Short-Term (Revenue Foundation - January)
1. Subscription tiers (Free/Pro/Premium)
2. Stripe integration
3. Feature gating
4. Playbook marketplace

### Medium-Term (Transaction Revenue - Q1)
1. Fundraising platform (compete with GoFundMe)
2. Digital game tickets (compete with Ticketmaster)
3. Private coaching bookings (compete with CoachUp)

### Long-Term (Marketplace & AI - Q2+)
1. NIL marketplace
2. League management system
3. AI highlight generator
4. AI stats from video
